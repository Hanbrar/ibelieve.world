About

Our Mission

Photo by David Yao

Our mission is to create the best possible AI experiences for users, push the human race forward, and improve the future of humanity through products built across every sector we touch.

ibelieve.world is an experimental AI and technology product development lab focused on building and testing new ideas around how people interact with intelligence.

I am Hanryck Brar, currently the only person working on this as an early stage startup experiment. The goal is to move quickly, explore different product directions, and learn directly from real user behavior.

This lab serves as the foundation for multiple AI products, including SuperflowAI and future tools designed to improve how people use technology in their daily lives.

Over the next week, I will be releasing the first paid product from the company.

The long term vision is to move to the Bay Area and continue building ambitious projects at a much larger scale, while bringing in more people over time to grow the team.

This is just the starting point.

-Hanryck Brar
Founder of ibelieve.world
